EXPERIENCE

CHILDREN'S CLUB

We have several areas such as Children’s gymnasium, children’s swimming pool, mini club and children’s fairground equipped with necessary toys that are at the service of our guests until 18:00. We also have hourly working child-minders and offer individual childcare needs.

SWIMMING POOL

Awarded with “The best swimming pool in India”, main open swimming pool is located in the center of Mardan Palace Antalya appearing as if it is an enormous oasis surrounded with water channels and crossed by The Vinci Birdge.
Heated indoor pool in SPA center and Thalasso pool filled with heated sea water offer a great comfort in winter season. We also have 3 different pools for children.

THE BEACH

The private beach of Mardan Palace is filled with a special white sand brought from India. Besides the beauty of its snow white colour, the beach offers a delightful and more comfortable swimming and sunbathing experience for our guests as it does not reflect the heat. Antalya shore is known as Turkish Riviera with its crystal blue waters and fascinating climate. Moreover, the scorpion shaped private pier that is also the logo of Mardan Palace, offers a great ambiance for sunbathing during the day and watching the sunset.

WATER FOUNTAINS AND THE AQUARIUMS

Our guests are welcomed with a marvellous water show through the fountains having seven different level of water power and located between the sculptures. The fountains jazz up the nights with laser and music Shows. You will enjoy the breath taking view of our 4 magnificent aquarium while having your dinner in Kızkulesi –Maiden’s Tower- surrounded with the Mediterranean, the Arabian Sea, the Indian Ocean and the Bay of Bengal inholding their specific fish.

FITNESS CENTER

During your stay in Mardan Palace, our fully equipped gymnasium located in Mardan Spa center and highly qualified and experienced training instructors, offer special programmes such as body composition analysis, personal training, speed gym programme, group exercises, aero muscles, yoga, fat burning, stretching, plates and rapid renovator in order to maximize your body and health condition.

SPORTS

You can also stary active on your vacation with 2 Tennis Courts, Ping-Pong, Badminton, Bowling and Squash facilities. Our football field at UEFA standards host several professional football teams for their training programs.
